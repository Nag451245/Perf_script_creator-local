What runs where, and what talks to what

One Node process per run, on your machine. It drives a local JMeter against your own environment, reads the correlation engine without ever writing to it, and calls an external service only if you turn AI on.

Everything inside the dashed boundary is one Node process, started fresh per run — kill it and nothing is left behind. The only outbound traffic is to your own environment, unless you switch AI assist on. Secrets live in perfscript.config.json, which is gitignored and scrubbed out of every stored recording.
